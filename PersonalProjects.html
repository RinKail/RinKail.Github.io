CyberScape

Tags: UE4, C++, Blueprints, Room-Generation Tools, Modular Weapons, Event based item systems

A Top-Down Shooter Bullet-Hell Roguelike. Cyberscape is my first attempt at creating a game from start to finish and is currently in the final stages of development, with small amounts of content remaining.
The main points of interest for this project are both on the Designer side and on the Player Side:

Room Creation in this project is incredibly fast as rooms are generating using a C++ powered Blueprint system that will create the rooms primary structure from a handful of vector values supplied by the designer.

On the other hand, the focus for players is on the item system. (I've heard that before) As the player progresses through the game, they can find modules to customize how their weapon functions, these will completly change the weapon's feel such as changing the weapon's projectile paths, replacing the projectiles themselves and even adding unique mechanics, such as slow fire rate, but every third shot does drastically increased damage.

Alongside the weapon system is the passive system. This utilises an event system that can, based on gameplay events trigger various mechanics. These can also trigger other specific events inside other items. As the player plays through the game, Due to how specialised the items are in this game, the drops will be customised to their current loadout while still providing branching options in the form of pure random items as additional item when it comes to select the next powerful item.

BattleBots (PlaceholderName)

Tags: UE5, C++, Blueprints, Modular Characters, Event based item-systems, movement Mechanics

A Platformer Third-Person Shooter where the player takes control of a Modular Robot.
Items take the form of phyiscal body parts(Gameplay defining effects) and software upgrades (Small gameplay effects).
Movement is a primary focus of the game, allowing players to ride on walls/Rails and Slide for quick speed-boosts to get out of danger.
This project is primarly for me to improve my capabiltiies in regards to the implementation of Animations and creating more complex gameplay oriented AI. Of course, I'm also hoping to produce a fun game!
