About Me

Hello! I'm Sam, a Games programmer based in England.

I've been developing games for the past 6 years, primarily working on personal projects alongside Education and have recently graduated from Staffordshire University. Here you can see some of the projects I've been working on; Whether they be personal or Academic.

My main engine of choice is Unreal Engine 4 and I have 5 years of experience using both C++ and Blueprints, using both to bring about the desired effects required. my Final-Year Project at University was also a tool built within the engine. Of course, with the release of UE5 I will now be using that instead!
